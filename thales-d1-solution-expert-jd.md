\# Technical Presales – Solution Expert (Thales D1 Platform)

\#\# Job title  
Technical Presales – Solution Expert, Thales D1 Modern Card Issuing Platform

\#\# Location  
Singapore – Banking & Payment Services, Customer Engagement & Success (CE\&S)

\---

\#\# About the role

As a Solution Expert you support the growth of Thales’ D1 modern card issuing platform by owning the technical stream of customer engagements from presales to early delivery.

You act as the technical partner to Sales and Customer Success, understand customer requirements for card issuing and digital wallets, design solutions based on D1 capabilities, and ensure continuity between what is proposed and what is delivered.

You work closely with product owners, architects and delivery teams to position D1, build demos and proof of concepts, and support strategic onboarding projects.

\---

\#\# Key responsibilities

\- Participate in qualification, discovery and presales activities for D1 opportunities with banks, fintechs and other issuers.  
\- Analyse customer business and technical requirements for card issuing, tokenisation and digital wallets.  
\- Design target solutions based on D1, including integration with issuer cores, processors and customer channels.  
\- Prepare and deliver technical presentations, workshops and demonstrations tailored to customer use cases.  
\- Contribute to responses to RFIs and RFPs, including the technical sections of proposals, solution descriptions and effort estimates.  
\- Build, maintain and operate demo environments and proof of concepts showcasing D1 features and use cases.  
\- Support Customer Success Managers during onboarding of key customers, acting as a bridge between presales commitments and project delivery.  
\- Capture and structure feedback from customers and prospects and feed it into product management and engineering.  
\- Share best practices, reference architectures and reusable assets with other Solution Experts and sales teams.  
\- Contribute to internal enablement on D1 capabilities, roadmap and positioning in the market.

\---

\#\# About you

\- Proven experience in a technical presales, solution consulting, solution architecture or similar customer facing role.  
\- Solid understanding of payments with strong focus on:  
  \- Card issuing (credit, debit, prepaid)  
  \- Digital wallets and tokenisation (Apple Pay, Google Pay and other OEM pays)  
  \- Card scheme interactions at a conceptual level  
\- Comfortable working with API based platforms and cloud environments:  
  \- REST APIs, JSON payloads, basic security concepts (keys, certificates, OAuth style flows)  
  \- Integration patterns between SaaS platforms, issuer cores, processors and digital channels  
\- Ability to translate business needs into technical requirements and high level architectures.  
\- Strong communication and presentation skills:  
  \- Able to explain complex topics clearly to both business and technical audiences  
  \- Confident in running workshops and demos on site and remotely  
\- Collaborative mindset and ability to work with international and cross functional teams (Sales, CSM, Product, Delivery).  
\- Fluent in English; other Asian languages are a plus.  
\- Willingness to travel in the region for customer meetings and internal events (up to roughly one third of the time).

\---

\#\# What we offer

\- Opportunity to work on a leading modern card issuing platform used by banks and fintechs globally.  
\- Exposure to a wide variety of customers and use cases in cards and digital wallets.  
\- Collaboration with experienced teams in Sales, Customer Success, Product Management and Engineering.  
\- International environment with strong focus on innovation, security and digital payments.
